Colorado River:
Rafting down the Colorado River is a beautiful and fun time. Floating through canyons with red stone walls on either side is a majestic and peaceful prelude to bouncing down rapids downriver!

Salmon River:
On this trip, you have the great opportunity to fish for salmon! Whitewater Rafting provides some fishing rods and equipment, but supplies are limited. If you have it, you are welcome to bring your own fishing rod and equipment. Enjoy rafting and fishing all in one trip!

Snake River:
What a ride the Snake River is! This twisting, turning river has great rapids and vistas. Sometimes you can see animals peeking out of the forest trees on the bank. Just around the river bend is a new adventure!
